Question: write three. point about iso
Answer: 1. ISO is the International Organization for Standardization, a worldwide federation of national standards bodies.
2. ISO develops and publishes International Standards that provide requirements, specifications, guidelines, or characteristics for products, services, and systems to ensure quality, safety, and efficiency.
3. ISO standards are voluntary, but they are often adopted by governments and businesses as a way to ensure that products and services meet certain minimum requirements.
